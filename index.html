How It Works

1. Photo

Your customers take a photo of your products and upload it to Instagram with a brand hashtag

2. Analyse

We pull in and analyse the photo for its sentiment and brand advocacy

3. Reward

If it meets your criteria, we comment on their photo with a link to a bespoke reward code

4. Redeem

Your customer verifies they took the photo and redeems the code on their next visit
